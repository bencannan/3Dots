Our Story

A few years ago, we had an idea we truly believed in. We spent almost two years talking about it, refining it, and planning it. Then we finally started building it. After a year of building, we validated the idea — and realized it was not going to work.

The hardest part was not the failure. It was realizing we could have tested the idea much earlier. If we had focused only on the core features needed to validate the solution, we could have saved months of time, money, and energy. Instead, we learned that lesson the hard way.

That experience changed how we think about building products.

And that's where 3Dots was born — so founders can validate faster, move smarter, and never lose years learning what they could know in weeks.

Our Mission

To help startup founders execute faster by building focused MVPs that validate ideas early and reduce the cost of uncertainty.

Our Vision

To create a startup ecosystem where founders learn sooner, build smarter, and never lose months—or years—waiting to validate an idea.

Our Team

We are a lean team of creative entrepreneurs, experienced software engineers, product designers, developers, and interns—creative, fast, and fun people teamed up for a unified vision.

We keep the team small on purpose. Fewer people means less overhead, clearer communication, and faster decisions. Everyone contributes directly to building and shipping—no silos, no long approval chains. We believe the best ideas come from a mix of disciplines: engineering rigor, design intuition, and founder-level ownership.

We work in tight loops: validate quickly, build in sprints, and learn from real users. Whether you're a seasoned engineer, a designer who loves prototyping, or an intern ready to learn by doing, you're here to move the needle. We value curiosity, speed, and a willingness to own outcomes from idea to launch.
